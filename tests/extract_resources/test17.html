Acamar Films Limited (the “Issuer” or the “Company”) is a company incorporated in England and Wales with registered number 04399568, and having its registered office at at 7 Savoy Court, London, United Kingdom, WC2R 0EX. Acamar Films Limited (“Acamar”) is a production and character licensing company based in Camden, London.
